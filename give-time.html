Give Time

What if we could offer time as a gesture?

A moment of attention.

A shared silence.

A temporary clearing.

Give Time is an invitation to slow down, to enter a shared moment of presence and communion, and to experience art as a living situation rather than as an object

Through ceremony, movement, installation, and collective attention, the work creates spaces for stillness, transformation, and encounter.

Show

A performative installation or spatial intervention conceived for a particular site.

A temporary environment composed with handmade votive candles or sculpture, flowers, salt, sand, textiles, or other materials, creating a space of attention and transformation.

Ceremony

A guided collective experience combining performance, movement, stillness, and ritual forms.

The ceremony may include the dancer Shin Shendo Weber and its practices, dance, silence, gesture, and scenographic elements, offering participants a moment of pause, presence, and shared awareness.
