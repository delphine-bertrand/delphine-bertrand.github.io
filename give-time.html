Give Time

A commissioned artistic proposition conceived for a specific context, audience, or place.

Show

A performative installation or spatial intervention conceived for a particular site.

A temporary environment composed with handmade votive candles, sculpture, flowers, salt, sand, textiles, or other natural materials, creating a space of attention and transformation.

Ceremony

A guided collective experience combining performance, movement, stillness, and ritual forms.

The ceremony may include Shin Shendo practice, dance, silence, gesture, and scenographic elements, offering participants a moment of pause, presence, and shared awareness.
