OFFER: Give time Details


What if I started to trust myself… what difference would it make if I gave more importance, value and attention to my intuition?

Rather than listening to the outside world and responding to its clamour, what if I finally tried to sense and then hear my own voice, my own breath, my needs, my limits and my desires?

That is where my strength comes from, my source.

This does not mean losing interest in the world, but rather embracing it fully, becoming part of it, and acting within it with greater awareness.

Have faith in awareness. Give time, offer time.
